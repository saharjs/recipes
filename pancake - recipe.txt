Ingriance 
1 cup flour 
1 cup milk
1 egg
1 ts baking soda
2 ts oil
1/8 ts sault 
3 ts sugaur 

Instruction
1. Add flour, sualt, sugaur and baking soda in one bowl and mix it well
2. Add egg and milk and oil in another bowl and wisk it well
3. Sift bowl number one to bowl number two and mix it well
4. Make the pan hot and put some butter on the surface of the pan 
5. fry each side for 3 min
